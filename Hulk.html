The Hulk

The Strongest Avenger

Biography

The Hulk, also known as Dr. Bruce Banner, is one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe. After being exposed to high levels of gamma radiation during an experiment gone wrong, Banner transforms into the Hulk when he becomes angry or stressed. While Banner is a brilliant scientist, the Hulk is known for his brute strength, resilience, and near-unstoppable rage.

Powers and Abilities

  • Superhuman Strength: The Hulk possesses virtually limitless strength, increasing proportionally with his anger.
  • Invulnerability: He has an extraordinary resistance to injury, withstanding extreme physical damage, radiation, and even magical attacks.
  • Regeneration: The Hulk can heal rapidly from injuries that would be fatal to most beings.
  • Limitless Stamina: His stamina is effectively boundless as his body generates no fatigue toxins.

Affiliations

The Hulk is a founding member of the Avengers and has fought alongside heroes such as Iron Man, Thor, and Captain America. Despite his sometimes uncontrollable nature, his power and bravery make him an essential part of the team. Bruce Banner also collaborates with other brilliant minds like Tony Stark and Reed Richards to solve some of the world's most challenging scientific problems.
